WOMEVERSE
seeks to unite women, non-binary people, and other underrepresented minorities into Web3! Our community is open to anyone, regardless of gender or sexual orientation.

Our values are embodied through education, a craving for fashion innovation, art, sustainability, and empowerment.

educa tion

We believe that education is essential in removing existing gender bias from both Web3 and the world in general.

For minorities, new space often feels intimidating, and Web3 is no exception. Its unfamiliar jargon and lack of diversity may dissuade some of us from joining.

Womeverse aims to turn your Web3 self-development process into a safe journey within a diverse, welcoming community of content creators.

Check out the first issue of the Womeverse Magazine to get a snippet of our various educational initiatives!

ART

Inspired by the history of woman's empowerment, we created an NFT collection to celebrate women, non-binary people, and other underrepresented minorities and to highlight their potential as creators in the Web3 space.

To honor their history and legacy, we are helping artists expand their reach through our diverse and inclusive platform.

As part of this initiative, the WMVRS Art Gallery & Marketplace will issue collaborative art drops with artists four times per year.

FASHION INNOVATION

It is estimated that by 2030, digital fashion will have replaced about 20% of physical garment production, decreasing our resource consumption and environmental impact.

Womeverse supports digital designers by collaborating, promoting, and selling their work in our exclusive online WMVRS Boutique.

Through our WMVRS meta closet, we are turning digital fashion into a social experience and paving the way for fashion-centered community initiatives in the metaverse.

SUPPORT

It may seem that society needs too much change. But this is not the case. While others may dream and remain complacent, we act. In order to create a better world for the future, change must start now.

The body of our Womeverse community is our Web3 curious and native friends who are here to find the right path to financial independence and new possibilities, helping us to build a new all-inclusive bright future.

Together, we are the soul of this project, carrying out common values and vision for our future.
