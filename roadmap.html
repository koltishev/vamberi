ROADMAP

Womeverse PFP Collection Launch

  • 10k Womeverse PFP collection by Artist: Ginevra Grigolo.
  • Minting details and instructions to be determined and announced soon.
  • Our first 20 early supporters will be airdropped Womeverse NFTs.
  • Creation of community wallet.
  • 5% of the total sales will be donated to causes we support.

WMVRS University Launch

  • We believe WOMEVERSE Magazine will help everyone understand blockchain and Web3, better express themselves, and learn through effective, easy-to-follow educational content.
  • All holders of WMVRS PFP Collection tokens will get primary access and additional issues of the Magazine.
  • Stay tuned for the launch of our future learning pathways program.

Womeverse PFP Collection Launch

  • WMVRS is community brand. Our goal is to collaborate with new and up-and-coming designers, help them showcase their talent, and make their work available to the Womeverse community.
  • Our top supporters will be airdropped Exclusive Gift Boxes that will delight holders with content and design.
  • Special offers and discounts will also be available to all our holders.

WMVRS Art Gallery&Marketplace

  • Our art platform will include a gallery and a marketplace designed to promote talented artists from the Womeverse community.
  • Our initial NFT drop will be Zodiac NFTs. Holders will be gifted prints and an annual personal zodiac forecast.
  • 10% of total sales will be donated to causes we support. Stay tuned to learn more

WMVRS Meta Closet Launch

  • We have ambitious goals to collaborate with expert fashion designers in bringing new names to the digital fashion universe and contribute to its mass adoption.
  • We will support and promote digital designers from our community by offering them a privileged space on our platform.
  • We will release a special issue of the WMVRS Magazine - the “Sustainability digest” - exploring the future of digital fashion and explaining our ideas and action framework in more detail.
  • Unique opportunities will be available to our holders!
