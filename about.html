OUR VISION

Contemporary women living in the free world are presented with a new world of possibilities when compared to what could be their experience in previous centuries.

Women living before the liberation movements were relegated to secondary roles by society. However, we trailblazed our way into the position we find ourselves in today as leaders and successful creatives.

In a poll held by our team, we asked women from our community to choose ten historical or fictional characters ranging from politicians, social activists, cartoon heroines, and any other figure who has changed their understanding of a woman's role in our world.

Our collection is inspired by the women who have changed the world, and continue to do so. Each image serves as a symbol of the vast knowledge and values that constitute us today.

Above all, we appreciate the endless struggles and contributions of women, non-binary people, and other underrepresented minorities who have forever shaped our history, culture, and values. Together, they are the co-designers of the world we see today and the future world we dream of.

MISSION

We want to build fundamentally equal possibilities for women and unrepresented minorities during the early stage of blockchain adoption.

Our mission is to fight the fear and misconceptions about Web3 by showing the value and possibilities of blockchain technology and inspiring every crypto-curious person to develop their skills and ideas in our ever-changing world.

We have deep thoughts, ideas, beliefs, and values about the world, and we also have the power to speak these truths and fight back when we are silenced.
